IT is a field of computing that is used daily in our lives. It is heavily used in business to store, transmit, manipulate, and receive all kinds of information. Within IT, there is a wide array of topics such as hardware, operating systems, and softwares. All of the subtopics in this field have evolved into an even more advanced and complex technology. IT is important for many reasons. It is almost impossible for a business to be able to succeed without the use of IT, that is, collecting important bits of data and turning them into something meaningful. Organizations now have dedicated IT departments to help ensure their success. There are more and more innovations coming to light which shows that IT is an ever evolving field.
